Smart e-Archive System

API-first document archiving backend for organizations that need structured storage, permissions, approval workflows, OCR extraction, and searchable archives.

Overview

The system is built as a Laravel 12 backend focused on document lifecycle management. It supports authentication, organization and department scopes, role-based authorization, document upload and retrieval, workflow history, OCR extraction, and search.

Problem

Organizations often store documents in scattered folders without clear ownership, approval status, access rules, or searchable extracted text. This makes document retrieval slow and creates operational risk.

Solution

  • API-first backend under protected REST endpoints.
  • Role-based access for SuperAdmin, Admin, Manager, Employee, and Auditor.
  • Organization and department-aware document access.
  • Document workflow states: pending, under review, approved, rejected, archived.
  • OCR extraction for image and PDF documents using Tesseract and PDF-to-image conversion.
  • Rule-based document classification into invoices, contracts, reports, official letters, IDs, receipts, certificates, legal documents, and other types.
  • Keyword search with optional hybrid semantic search when embeddings are configured.
